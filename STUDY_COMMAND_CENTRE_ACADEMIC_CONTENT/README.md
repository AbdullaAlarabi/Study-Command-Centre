# MKT112 & MGT112 Study Command Centre — Approved Academic Content Pack

This folder is the academic backbone for the private study website. It contains the actual chapter revision content, chapter quiz banks, full revision packs, and three mock exams for each midterm and final assessment block.

## Non-negotiable use rule

Codex must **import and display this content**. It must not replace, expand, paraphrase, or supplement it with general management or marketing knowledge. The supplied university slides are the source of truth. The questions are deliberately written in the direct Arab Open University style shown in the uploaded revision sheet and practice examinations.

## Folder map

- `00_governance/` — content rules, source basis, integration prompt, and manifest.
- `01_course_overview/` — assessment structure and content roadmap.
- `02_chapter_packs/` — student-facing revision notes for all twelve chapters.
- `03_question_banks/` — twelve chapter banks. Each bank contains 12 MCQs and 4 essay questions with answers and marking points.
- `04_revision_packs/` — full midterm/final revision centres for MKT112 and MGT112.
- `05_mock_exams/` — student-facing papers for three mocks per assessment block.
- `06_mock_answer_keys/` — answer keys and essay marking guides. These must never appear on the student mock screen before submission.

## Website behaviour

1. The student studies a chapter pack.
2. The student clicks **Complete Chapter**.
3. The system generates 5 MCQs from that chapter bank plus 1 essay question.
4. The chapter receives provisional completion only when the student scores at least 4/5 on the MCQs and submits the essay.
5. The coach can review the essay separately. The build may initially treat essay submission as sufficient to avoid blocking progress.
6. After all three chapters in an assessment block are completed, the revision pack unlocks.
7. Mock 1, Mock 2, and Mock 3 then unlock sequentially.

## Important MKT112 assumption

No official MKT112 past paper was supplied. Therefore, the MKT112 mock format uses the same clear AOU pattern evidenced by the supplied management revision/practice material:

- Midterm mocks: 5 MCQs + 2 structured essays.
- Final mocks: 5 MCQs + 3 structured essays.

This is a documented design assumption, not a claim about an official unpublished MKT112 paper.
