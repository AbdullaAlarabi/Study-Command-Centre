## Question 2: Model answer and marking guide

**Question:** Explain the three main sources of marketing information and compare the main primary-data research approaches.

**Model answer**

Marketing information comes from internal databases, competitive marketing intelligence, and marketing research. Internal databases contain company information such as sales, costs, customer records, inventory, and web activity. Competitive marketing intelligence systematically collects publicly available information about consumers, competitors, and the environment. Marketing research gathers data for a specific situation. Primary research approaches include observation, which studies actual behavior; survey, which asks about knowledge, attitudes, preferences, or behavior; and experiment, which uses treatments and controls to study cause and effect.

**Marking points**

- Internal databases
- Competitive marketing intelligence
- Marketing research
- Observation
- Survey
- Experiment
- Correct distinctions and purpose

**Source:** MKT112 Chapter 4, information sources and research approaches

## Question 3: Model answer and marking guide

**Question:** Compare customer value-based, cost-based, and competition-based pricing.

**Model answer**

Customer value-based pricing begins with buyers’ perceptions of value and sets a price that captures that value. Cost-based pricing begins with the costs of producing, distributing, and selling the product and adds a return. Competition-based pricing considers competitors’ strategies, costs, prices, and market offers. Value-based pricing is customer driven, cost-based pricing is product driven, and competition-based pricing uses the competitive market as the main reference. A sound final price usually considers all three perspectives.

**Marking points**

- Value-based definition
- Cost-based definition
- Competition-based definition
- Customer-driven distinction
- Product-driven distinction
- Competitor reference
- Clear comparison

**Source:** MKT112 Chapter 10, major pricing strategies
