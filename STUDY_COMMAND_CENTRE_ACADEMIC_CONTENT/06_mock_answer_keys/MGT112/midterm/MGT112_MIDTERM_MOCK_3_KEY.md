## Question 2: Model answer and marking guide

**Question:** Discuss the main employee-selection devices and explain how interviews can be improved.

**Model answer**

Selection devices include written tests, performance-simulation tests, work sampling, assessment centers, and interviews. Written tests measure relevant abilities or knowledge. Work sampling reproduces routine job tasks, while assessment centers simulate managerial problems. Interviews are improved when they are structured, use standardized and job-related questions, apply consistent evaluation criteria, train interviewers, and avoid irrelevant personal judgments. Selection devices should be reliable and valid.

**Marking points**

- Written tests
- Performance simulations
- Work sampling
- Assessment centers
- Interviews
- Structured/standardized questions
- Job relevance and consistent evaluation
- Reliability and validity

**Source:** MGT112 Chapter 8, selection section

## Question 3: Model answer and marking guide

**Question:** Compare a work group and a work team, and explain the main components of an effective team.

**Model answer**

A work group mainly shares information and helps individual members perform, while a work team works intensely on a common goal and creates positive synergy through complementary skills and individual and mutual accountability. Effective teams require a supportive context, suitable composition, motivating work design, and effective processes. Context includes resources, leadership, trust, and rewards. Composition includes abilities, roles, diversity, size, flexibility, and preferences. Work design includes autonomy, skill variety, a whole task, and significance. Processes include common purpose, specific goals, efficacy, useful task conflict, and reduced social loafing.

**Marking points**

- Correct group/team distinction
- Positive synergy
- Complementary skills
- Mutual accountability
- Context
- Composition
- Work design
- Processes
- Examples under each component

**Source:** MGT112 Chapter 9, team sections
