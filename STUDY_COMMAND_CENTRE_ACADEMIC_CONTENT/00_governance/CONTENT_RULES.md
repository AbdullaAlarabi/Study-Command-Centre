# Academic Content Rules

1. **Slides only:** Every examinable fact must be supported by the supplied chapter slides or the course syllabus/revision sheet.
2. **No external enrichment:** Do not add theories, definitions, examples, calculations, or terminology from the internet or another textbook edition.
3. **Preserve course wording:** Key definitions and named models should remain close to the wording used in the slides.
4. **AOU question style:** Use clear definitions, distinctions, lists, processes, short applications, and structured essays. Avoid trick questions.
5. **MCQ options:** Exactly four options, one best answer, no “all of the above,” and no ambiguous answer pairs.
6. **Chapter quiz gate:** Randomly select 5 MCQs and 1 essay from the relevant chapter bank. Passing threshold is 4/5 MCQs plus essay submission.
7. **Mock integrity:** Each fixed mock contains unique questions within its assessment block. Do not show answer keys before submission.
8. **Source labels:** Source slide references are internal audit metadata. They do not need to be visible to the student.
9. **MKT exam format:** Treat the supplied MKT format as an implementation assumption because no official paper was provided.
10. **Content editing:** Any future content correction must preserve stable item IDs so historical attempts remain valid.
