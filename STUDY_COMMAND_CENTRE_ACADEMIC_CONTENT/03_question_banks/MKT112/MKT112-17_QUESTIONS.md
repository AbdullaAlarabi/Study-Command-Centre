## Essay bank
### MKT112-C17-ESSAY-01
- **Topic:** Digital marketing
- **Source slides:** 5-8
- **Question:** Define digital marketing and explain its benefits to buyers and sellers.

**Model answer**

Digital marketing uses internet, mobile, social, and other technology-intensive platforms to engage targeted individuals, communities, and businesses. Buyers gain convenience, privacy, information, comparison, immediacy, interactivity, and community. Sellers gain fast and low-cost reach, flexibility, personalization, relationship building, real-time marketing, and access to markets difficult to reach through traditional channels.

**Marking points**
- Definition
- Buyer benefits
- Seller benefits
- Relationship/real-time value

### MKT112-C17-ESSAY-02
- **Topic:** Campaign preparation
- **Source slides:** 10-12
- **Question:** Explain how marketers prepare for a digital campaign.

**Model answer**

Marketers create detailed consumer personas for each target segment, including needs, goals, habits, influences, concerns, and online behavior. They study how consumers navigate among digital and physical channels at each buying stage. They use experiments such as A/B tests to compare treatments, measure results, refine tactics, and reduce expensive mistakes while controlling experimental noise.

**Marking points**
- Persona
- Segment-specific use
- Omni-channel navigation
- Tracking challenge
- A/B testing
- Noise/control

### MKT112-C17-ESSAY-03
- **Topic:** Digital channels
- **Source slides:** 14-20
- **Question:** Discuss online, social media, and mobile marketing.

**Model answer**

Online marketing uses websites, display and search ads, email, video, and blogs. Marketing sites move customers toward outcomes, while community sites build engagement. Social media enables targeted, interactive, immediate, shareable relationships but is user controlled and can backfire. Mobile marketing reaches consumers anywhere through phones and apps and supports personalized, relevant, frictionless experiences.

**Marking points**
- Online forms
- Website types
- Social advantages
- Social challenges
- Mobile definition/benefits

### MKT112-C17-ESSAY-04
- **Topic:** Omni-channel and policy
- **Source slides:** 21-26
- **Question:** Define omni-channel marketing and discuss its challenges and public-policy issues.

**Model answer**

Omni-channel marketing integrates store, online, mobile, and other channels into one seamless buying experience. It requires alignment with the business model, incentives, organizational structures, and the full marketing mix. Public-policy issues include irritation, unfairness, deception, fraud, consumer privacy, data integrity, and security. Firms should provide transparency, choice, and responsible data use.

**Marking points**
- Definition
- Business model alignment
- Incentives/structure
- Marketing mix alignment
- Privacy
- Irritation/deception/fraud
- Transparency/security
